Word Count Guide

This guide explains recommended word counts for essays, blog posts, SEO articles, and professional writing.

SEO Word Counts

SEO articles perform best between 1,000 to 2,500 words depending on competition.

Blog Posts

High-performing blog posts average 1,500+ words.

Why Word Count Matters
