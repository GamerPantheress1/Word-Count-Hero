What is Word Count Hero?

Word Count Hero is a free online word counter built for writers, bloggers, students, and professionals who need accurate, instant text statistics. It counts words, characters with and without spaces, sentences, paragraphs, and estimates reading time in real time.

All analysis happens locally in your browser. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
